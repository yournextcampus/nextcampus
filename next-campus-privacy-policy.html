Effective Date: 12 August 2025

Next Campus (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), the Data Protection Act 2018, and other applicable laws.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with us (including via our Facebook Lead Ads, website forms, email, phone, or in person), we may collect the following information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Date of birth
  • Postcode and address
  • Course interest and preferred start date
  • Information relevant to Student Finance eligibility (e.g., residency status, previous study history)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Contact you regarding your enquiry or application
  • Provide free guidance on course selection and Student Finance
  • Assist you with applications to universities or colleges
  • Maintain our records and comply with legal obligations
  • Improve our services and marketing activities

3. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Your consent (when you complete a lead form or opt in to communications)
  • Performance of a contract (to provide services you request)
  • Legitimate interests (to operate and grow our business, in ways that do not override your rights)

4. Sharing Your Information

  • Partner universities, colleges, or training providers (to process your application)
  • Student Finance England or other relevant funding bodies (with your consent)
  • Technology and marketing service providers (e.g., CRM systems, email platforms) who help us deliver our services

We will never sell your data to third parties.

5. Data Retention

  • Leads who do not proceed: up to 12 months
  • Enrolled students: for the duration of their course plus any legally required retention period

6. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access a copy of your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Withdraw your consent at any time
